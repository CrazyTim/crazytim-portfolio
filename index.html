Job Scheduler - Buildsafe Australia
windows desktop app (2019)
vb.net,winforms,php,javascript,html-5,css-3,json,ms-sql,filemaker,google-directions-api,ui-design,dev-ops

I am currently the designer and lead programmer for this in-house desktop application. It is used daily by 60+ office staff and allows us to prepare and schedule work across our 100+ vehicles and 13+ depots.

I am responsible for keeping it running smoothly (monitoring, debugging), and I work with stakeholders to help scope new features, implement, test, and deploy.

Focus is placed on making it easy to use for non-technical staff. It is used across many departments such as; operations, drafting, auditing, factory, depot managers, finance, and training. In particular it alows you to show job and travel routes on a map, integrates with GPS data, facilitates picking of stock, and allows direct communication with customers via our website, SMS, and email templates.

Countdown
web animation (2018)
three.js,tween.js

A replication of the countdown animation in the film Mission to Mars. I had fun referencing the movie and learned a lot.

I love the design of this binary timer. The rings appear to be resting on a translucent surface, which gives a sense of depth as they rotate through it. I used many layers of transparency beneath the rings to create this effect. It was challenging to position the camera and perspective the same as the movie.

An interesting fact: the animation lasts about a minute, which wasn't actually long enough for the scene in the movie!

Voyager Episode Archive
prototype flash app (2003)
actionscript,animation,data-collection,data-entry,research,interface-design,audio

A functional Start Trek LCARS touch interface with sounds (sounds borrowed from a Start Trek computer game).

I examined many examples from the show to learn the design language. I wanted the design to be functional and meaningful, while at the same time fun to interact with.

Features include:

  • episode outline and full synopsis
  • category tags
  • stardate
  • star ratings
  • classification rating
  • and up to six photos per episode
  • Episodes can be navigated by season, topic, or chronological timeline
  • Search function and random episode selector
  • contextual help labels.
